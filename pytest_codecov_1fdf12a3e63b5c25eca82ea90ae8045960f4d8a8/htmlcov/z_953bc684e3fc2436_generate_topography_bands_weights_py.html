19 """Runs topographic weights generation. 

20 

21 Reads the orography and land_sea_mask fields of a cube. Creates a series of 

22 topographic zone weights to indicate where an orography point sits within 

23 the defined topographic bands. If the orography point is in the centre of 

24 a topographic band, then a single band will have a weight 1.0. 

25 If the orography point is at the edge of a topographic band, then the 

26 upper band will have a 0.5 weight whilst the lower band will also have a 

27 0.5 weight. Otherwise the weight will vary linearly between the centre of 

28 a topographic band and the edge. 

29 

30 Args: 

31 orography (iris.cube.Cube): 

32 The orography on a standard grid. 

33 land_sea_mask (iris.cube.Cube): 

34 Land mask on a standard grid, with land points set to one and 

35 sea points set to zero. If provided, sea points will be 

36 masked and set to the default fill value. If no land mask is 

37 provided, weights will be generated for sea points as well as land 

38 in the appropriate topographic band. 

39 bands_config (dict): 

40 Definition of orography bands required. 

41 The expected format of the dictionary is e.g 

42 {'bounds':[[0, 50], [50, 200]], 'units': 'm'} 

43 The default dictionary has the following form: 

44 {'bounds': [[-500., 50.], [50., 100.], 

45 [100., 150.],[150., 200.], [200., 250.], 

46 [250., 300.], [300., 400.], [400., 500.], 

47 [500., 650.],[650., 800.], [800., 950.], 

48 [950., 6000.]], 'units': 'm'} 

49 

50 Returns: 

51 iris.cube.Cube: 

52 Cube containing the weights depending upon where the orography 

53 point is within the topographical zones. 

54 """
